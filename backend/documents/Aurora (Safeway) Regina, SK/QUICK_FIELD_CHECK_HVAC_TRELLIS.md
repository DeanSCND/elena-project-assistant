## FIELD MEASUREMENTS NEEDED (4 QUICK CHECKS)

### 1. FIND FINISHED FLOOR TO DECK HEIGHT
**Who does it:** Anyone with a laser measure or tape

**How:**
- Stand in sales floor (middle of store, away from walls)
- Shoot laser straight up to underside of metal deck
- Write down number: **_______ feet**

**Expected:** Should be ~21'-6" to 22'-0"

---

### 2. MEASURE DUCT DROP FROM DECK
**Who does it:** Mechanical guy

**How:**
- Look at the **biggest** duct trunk (main supply from RTU)
- Measure from bottom of deck to bottom of duct
- Include the insulation wrap
- Write down: **_______ inches** (or ______ feet)

**Expected:** Probably 30"-48" (2.5' to 4')

---

### 3. CALCULATE BOTTOM OF DUCTWORK
**Math:**
- Take measurement #1 (deck height): _______ ft
- Subtract measurement #2 (duct drop): - _______ ft
- **= Bottom of duct from floor: _______ ft**

**Example:**
- Deck height: 22'-0"
- Duct drops: 3'-6"
- Bottom of duct = 18'-6" above floor

- **Top of trellis: ~16'-4"** above floor
