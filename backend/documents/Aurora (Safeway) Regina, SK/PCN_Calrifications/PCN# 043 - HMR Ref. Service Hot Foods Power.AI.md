## Executive Summary

This Proposed Change Notice corrects the power requirements for the HMR (Home Meal Replacement) service hot foods case equipment H-22. The equipment type has been revised from a "Self Service Hot Foods" case to a "Full Service Hot Foods" case, requiring significant electrical modifications including voltage changes and the addition of a separate receptacle circuit.

**Key Change**: Equipment H-22 voltage revised from 208V-3PH to 208V-1PH with circuit breaker modification from 70A-3P to 70A-2P, plus addition of a new 120V-1PH ISO outlet on separate 15A-1P circuit.
