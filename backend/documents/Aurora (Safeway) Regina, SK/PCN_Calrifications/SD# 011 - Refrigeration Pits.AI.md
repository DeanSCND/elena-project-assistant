### Installation Method Variance

**Contract Documents Expectation** (per A7.0 & S1.3):
- Liner securing rods cast into concrete
- Adjustable upward aluminum frame

**Proposed Manufacturer Method**:
1. Install refrigeration pits on granular base to set height and location
2. Screw screws into side of pit before pouring concrete (to prevent movement)
3. Pour concrete around the pit
4. Remove the frame

**Potential Issues**:
- Height adjustment capability may be limited compared to specified system
- No cast-in securing rods may affect pit stability
- Installation sequence differs from architectural/structural details
- Temporary screw fixings vs. permanent cast-in rods
