## 5. CODE COMPLIANCE NOTES

### Building Code Considerations

#### Stair Requirements
- **IBC/NBC Chapter 10 (Means of Egress)**:
  - Verify minimum stair width maintained (typically 44" for egress stairs, 36" minimum for accessory stairs)
  - Confirm handrail extends required length along stair run
  - Handrail height requirements: 34"-38" above stair nosing
  - Handrail must be continuous along full length of stair flight

#### Handrail Specifications (Code Requirements)
- **Graspability**: Circular cross-section 1.25" to 2" diameter OR perimeter 4" to 6.25"
- **Clearance**: Minimum 1.5" clear from wall surface to handrail
- **Structural**: Must withstand 200 lb concentrated load or 50 lb/linear foot
- **Returns**: Handrail ends must return to wall or terminate at newel post

#### Guard Requirements
