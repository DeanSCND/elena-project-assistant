ELECTRICAL PROPOSED CHANGE NOTICE #21 
 
24-258e 
 

Project: 
Aurora Grocery Store 
 
2000 Anaquod Road, Regina, Saskatchewan 
Subject: 
Correct power for requirements for service hot foods case 
Date: 
2025.10.19 
 

Subject: 
Correct power for requirements for service hot foods case 
References: 
Electrical Drawing E0.2 – Schedules 
 
Electrical Drawing E2.4 – Partial Layout - Power 
 

1. 
HMR equipment ‘H-22’ as shown on electrical drawing E2.4 is revised from a ‘Self Service Hot 
Foods’ to a ‘Full Service Hot Foods’ and requires two separate power sources – one for the case 
and one for a separate circuit receptacle. Refer to manufacturer’s literature, wire and connect as 
required. 

1.1. Voltage required is revised from 208V-3PH to 208V-1PH. Circuit breaker to be revised from 

a 70A-3P breaker to a 70A-2P breaker c/w #4 wire. 

1.2. Wire and connect power for one(1) 120V-1PH ISO outlet on a separate 15A-1P circuit 

breaker c/w #12 wire.
